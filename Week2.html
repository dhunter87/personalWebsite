What did go well?

Our final product and prototype turnued our very well and got good feedback. our final presentation also went very well.

What didn't go well?

We could have worked better as a team. In my oppinion, we, as a team, missed a crucial part of the SDLC cycle (ideation, recap and ideation). We enfed the day without a valueable product to work with. As a result we ended up behind schedule, the new concept we came up with was not collaborative and it took a long time and a lot of effort for the team to re-align.

What did I learn? What am I looking forwards to?

I learned that I can lead a team through a difficult moments when the team is not aligned. I learned that I should allow space for less dominant personalities to give theur input before myself for the benefit of the team. I also learned valuable presentation skills and gained valuable experience doing presentations.
